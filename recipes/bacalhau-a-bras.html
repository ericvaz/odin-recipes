Bacalhau à Brás

Description

A traditional Portuguese dish made with shredded codfish, onions, fried potatoes, and scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 400g salted codfish
  • 2 large potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 4 eggs
  • Olive oil
  • Black olives
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Soak the salted codfish overnight and then boil it. Shred it into small pieces.
  2. Cut the potatoes into thin strips and fry them until golden.
  3. In a pan, sauté the onions in olive oil until soft, then add the codfish.
  4. Add the fried potatoes and mix gently.
  5. Whisk the eggs and pour them into the pan. Stir until the eggs are cooked but creamy.
  6. Garnish with parsley and black olives. Serve warm.
