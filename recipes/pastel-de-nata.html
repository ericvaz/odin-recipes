Pastel de Nata

Description

A famous Portuguese custard tart with a flaky crust and creamy filling, enjoyed with a sprinkle of cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 200ml milk
  • 150g sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
