Caldo Verde

Description

A hearty green soup made with kale, potatoes, and chorizo, perfect for cold evenings.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 200g kale (finely chopped)
  • 1 chorizo sausage
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel and dice the potatoes, onion, and garlic.
  2. Boil them in a pot of salted water until soft.
  3. Blend the mixture until smooth to create a creamy soup base.
  4. Add the chopped kale and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Slice the chorizo and fry it lightly in olive oil, then add to the soup.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and serve hot.
